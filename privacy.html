Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 02/12/2025

Welcome to NettleWeb! We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This privacy policy governs the collection, use, and sharing of personal information by NettleWeb ("the Website") for the purposes of operating its services, including user login, data sharing, and chat features. The following terms and definitions apply unless otherwise noted. By using the Website, you agree to be bound by this policy. If you do not agree to its terms, you may not use the Website.

1. Information We Collect

The Website uses third-party analytics services to collect the following types of information:

  • Log Data: Data collected automatically by the Website, including but not limited to:
    • IP addresses
    • Browser type
    • Browser cookies
    • Device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Information about your use of the Website, including but not limited to:
    • Pages viewed
    • Links clicked
    • Time spent on the Website
  • Login Information: If you choose to register with us using Google OAuth login, we collect your Google login credentials, including your name, email address, and profile information.

You may provide additional information by consenting to our use of your data as described in this policy.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the Website.
  • To provide services to you, including user login, access to features, and chat services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect against unauthorized access, fraud, or theft.
  • To enforce agreements between you and the Website.
  • To contact you for billing or account-related matters.
  • To provide support and customer service.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve the Website.

We may share your information with third parties, including service providers, platform partners, and law enforcement agencies, as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above.

3. Data Sharing

The Website may use third-party services, including analytics, cookies, and chat platforms. These third parties may collect and use your information on our behalf.

We may aggregate your data with other users' data to improve our services, but we will not disclose individual user data in this manner.

4. Data Security

We are committed to securing your personal information through appropriate measures, including:

  • Data Encryption: Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your data.
  • Regular Updates: Our systems and technologies are regularly updated to patch vulnerabilities and protect against unauthorized access.

5. Advertising

We use advertising on our website to support our business. We display ads from third-party advertising networks, including Google AdSense. These ads are tailored to your interests based on the data we collect about you. If you have any concerns about advertising, please see the section on "Advertising Choices" below.

6. Google OAuth Login

If you choose to register with us using Google OAuth login, we collect your Google login credentials and profile information. We do not collect or store your Google login credentials, and you can remove your access to our website at any time by revoking our access to your Google account.

7. Advertising Choices

If you have concerns about advertising on our website, you can use the following options to opt-out:

  • Using a browser extension: You can install a browser extension that blocks advertising on our website.
  • Disabling cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser to prevent us from collecting data about your interactions with our website.
  • Contacting us: You can contact us directly with any concerns about advertising on our website.

8. Your Rights and Privacy

You have the following rights with respect to your data:

  • Access to Your Data: You may access, correct, or delete your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to certain types of data collection and processing activities.
  • Right to withdraw consent: If you consented to the collection and processing of your data, you may withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Right to Information: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Data Breach Notification: If your data is breach-eligible, we will notify you within 30 days of a breach.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be amended from time to time. Any amendments will be effective upon our posting of the revised policy on the Website.

We reserve the right to modify this policy to comply with changes in laws, regulations, or technological advancements.

10. Notes

This policy complies with the requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

This policy applies to all activities conducted by us in the Jurisdiction.

By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy. If you have any concerns or questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
